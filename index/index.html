Newton School has placed over 1,500 aspirants in over 600 companies including Google, Lenskart, Razorpay, Flipkart, Zomato, Deloitte, Meesho and Target in a little over two years.

Frequently asked questions

How long will it take to complete the course?

The course duration will be for 6 months.

How much should I pay and for how long?

You have to pay a sum of 2.5 Lakhs over a period of 36 months after getting placed in one of the companies offering a minimum guarantee of 5 LPA.

What is the eligibility to join the course?

Anyone who is a working professional with work experience, a graduate, as well as students currently studying in a college/university is eligible for the course.

Am I eligible for this this course if I'm from a non-technical background?

Yes, you're absolutely eligible to apply for our courses. You'll be trained deeply with the necessary technical skills so that you can choose technical streams as your career.

Is registration fees required to apply for the course?

You will be required to pay a fee of ₹5000/- for the admission to the course.

After getting a job from Newton school, if the company is found to be fraud and fires me after 3 months, do I still need to pay the fees?

As mentioned, we help students to get placed in the top-notch companies with good packages, there’s no chance of any fraudulent as such. We allow companies on the placement tracks only once they're testified.

Can someone who has 2 years gap after his graduation and doesn't have a job for this year, apply for the course?

Yes, absolutely!

Can I apply for Newton school even if I am not from India?

Yes, anyone who belongs to countries other than India can also apply for the course. You just need to have proper identity proof to apply for the same.

Is there is a requirement for Laptop?

Definately,Without laptop you can,t enrolled into our Course.

If I sign the PAPA can I cancel it?

Yes , You can take Your decision within a month only .After one month charges will be taken.
